Coorg

The Scotland of India

About Coorg

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a beautiful hill station in Karnataka. It is famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, waterfalls, forests and pleasant climate. Coorg is one of South India's most popular nature destinations.

Top Attractions

Abbey Falls

Beautiful waterfall surrounded by coffee estates.

Raja's Seat

Popular viewpoint offering stunning sunset views.

Dubare Elephant Camp

Interact with elephants and enjoy river activities.

Quick Facts

  • State: Karnataka
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Famous For: Coffee Plantations & Nature
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport
  • Languages: Kannada, English
