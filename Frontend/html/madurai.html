Madurai

The Temple City of Tamil Nadu

About Madurai

Madurai is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in India. Known as the Temple City, it is famous for the magnificent Meenakshi Amman Temple, rich Tamil culture, historical monuments, and vibrant local markets. Madurai is an important cultural and spiritual center of South India.

Top Attractions

Meenakshi Amman Temple

One of India's most famous and beautiful temples.

Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal

A grand palace showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Gandhi Memorial Museum

A museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi's life and legacy.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Famous For: Meenakshi Temple & Tamil Culture
  • Nearest Airport: Madurai Airport
  • Languages: Tamil, English
