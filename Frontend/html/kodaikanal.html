Kodaikanal

Princess of Hill Stations

About Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is one of Tamil Nadu's most popular hill stations. Located in the Western Ghats, it is known for its cool climate, beautiful valleys, lakes, waterfalls, forests and scenic views. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and families.

Top Attractions

Kodaikanal Lake

Famous star-shaped lake for boating and cycling.

Coaker's Walk

Scenic walking path offering breathtaking views.

Pillar Rocks

Massive rock formations and popular viewpoint.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time: April - June
  • Famous For: Lakes, Waterfalls & Hills
  • Nearest Airport: Madurai
  • Languages: Tamil, English
