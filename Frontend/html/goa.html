Goa

India's Beach Paradise

About Goa

Goa is India's most popular beach destination, known for its golden beaches, nightlife, Portuguese architecture, seafood and water sports. It attracts millions of tourists every year.

Top Attractions

Baga Beach

One of Goa's most famous beaches for nightlife and fun.

Dudhsagar Falls

Magnificent waterfall surrounded by lush forests.

Fort Aguada

Historic Portuguese fort overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Quick Facts

  • State: Goa
  • Best Time: November - February
  • Famous For: Beaches & Nightlife
  • Nearest Airport: Goa International Airport
  • Languages: Konkani, English, Hindi
