Tenkasi

The Gateway to Western Ghats

About Tenkasi

Tenkasi is a beautiful town in Tamil Nadu known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, temples and the majestic Western Ghats. It is home to the famous Kasi Viswanathar Temple and serves as a gateway to Courtallam and Papanasam.

Top Attractions

Kasi Viswanathar Temple

Historic temple famous for its magnificent tower.

Courtallam Falls

Popular waterfalls located near Tenkasi.

Western Ghats

Beautiful mountain ranges with breathtaking views.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time: June - February
  • Famous For: Temples & Waterfalls
  • Nearest Railway Station: Tenkasi Junction
  • Languages: Tamil, English
