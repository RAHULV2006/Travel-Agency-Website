Papanasam

The Natural Paradise of Tirunelveli

About Papanasam

Papanasam is a beautiful tourist destination located in the Tirunelveli district of Tamil Nadu. Surrounded by the Western Ghats, it is famous for waterfalls, dams, rivers, forests and temples. The place offers a peaceful environment for nature lovers and spiritual travelers.

Top Attractions

Agasthiyar Falls

Beautiful waterfall surrounded by greenery.

Papanasam Dam

A scenic dam located amidst mountains and forests.

Papanasanathar Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • District: Tirunelveli
  • Best Time: June - February
  • Famous For: Waterfalls, Dam & Nature
  • Languages: Tamil, English
