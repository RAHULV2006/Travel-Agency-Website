Alleppey

Venice of the East

About Alleppey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is one of Kerala's most famous tourist destinations. It is known for its picturesque backwaters, houseboat cruises, beaches, lagoons and lush green landscapes.

Top Attractions

Alleppey Backwaters

Enjoy the world-famous houseboat cruises.

Alleppey Beach

A beautiful beach with a historic sea pier.

Vembanad Lake

The largest lake in Kerala with scenic views.

Quick Facts

  • State: Kerala
  • Best Time: November - February
  • Famous For: Houseboats & Backwaters
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport
  • Languages: Malayalam, English
