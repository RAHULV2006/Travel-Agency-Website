Courtallam

The Spa of South India

About Courtallam

Courtallam, located in Tenkasi District of Tamil Nadu, is famous for its natural waterfalls and herbal-rich waters. Surrounded by the Western Ghats, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations in South India.

Top Attractions

Main Falls

The most famous waterfall attracting thousands of visitors.

Five Falls

Unique waterfall that splits into five streams.

Old Courtallam Falls

Peaceful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • District: Tenkasi
  • Best Time: June - September
  • Famous For: Waterfalls & Herbal Baths
  • Languages: Tamil, English
