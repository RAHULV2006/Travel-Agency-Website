Kanyakumari

The Southern Tip of India

About Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is the southernmost point of India, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal and Indian Ocean meet. It is famous for its beautiful sunrise and sunset views, Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue.

Top Attractions

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

Historic monument built on a rocky island.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

133-feet tall statue of Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar.

Sunrise Point

One of the best sunrise viewing locations in India.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Famous For: Sunrise & Sunset
  • Nearest Airport: Trivandrum
  • Languages: Tamil, English
