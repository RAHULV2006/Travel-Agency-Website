Munnar

Nature's Paradise in Kerala

About Munnar

Munnar is a beautiful hill station located in Kerala's Western Ghats. It is famous for its endless tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and cool climate. Munnar is one of the most visited tourist destinations in South India.

Top Attractions

Tea Gardens

Vast green tea plantations covering the hills.

Eravikulam National Park

Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.

Mattupetty Dam

A scenic reservoir surrounded by hills and forests.

Quick Facts

  • State: Kerala
  • Best Time: September - March
  • Famous For: Tea Plantations & Hill Views
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport
  • Languages: Malayalam, English
