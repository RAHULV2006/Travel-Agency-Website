Sri Lanka

The Pearl of the Indian Ocean

About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island nation known for its golden beaches, tea plantations, ancient temples, wildlife sanctuaries and rich cultural heritage. It is one of the most popular international tourist destinations near India.

Top Attractions

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

UNESCO World Heritage Site with breathtaking views.

Kandy

Famous cultural city and home of the Temple of the Tooth.

Galle Fort

Historic fort built during the Portuguese and Dutch eras.

Quick Facts

  • Country: Sri Lanka
  • Capital: Colombo
  • Best Time: December - April
  • Famous For: Beaches, Tea Estates & Wildlife
  • Languages: Sinhala, Tamil, English
