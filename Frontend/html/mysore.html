Mysore

The Cultural Capital of Karnataka

About Mysore

Mysore is one of Karnataka's most famous tourist destinations. Known for its royal heritage, magnificent palaces, gardens, temples and vibrant Dasara festival, Mysore attracts visitors from around the world.

Top Attractions

Mysore Palace

One of India's most beautiful royal palaces.

Chamundi Hills

Famous hilltop temple with panoramic city views.

Brindavan Gardens

Beautiful gardens known for musical fountain shows.

Quick Facts

  • State: Karnataka
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Famous For: Palace, Culture & Dasara Festival
  • Nearest Airport: Mysore Airport
  • Languages: Kannada, English
