Rameswaram

The Sacred Island of India

About Rameswaram

Rameswaram is one of the most sacred pilgrimage destinations in India. Located on Pamban Island in Tamil Nadu, it is famous for the Ramanathaswamy Temple, beautiful beaches, Pamban Bridge, and its connection to the Ramayana.

Top Attractions

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples in India.

Pamban Bridge

India's first sea bridge with breathtaking views.

Dhanushkodi

Historic ghost town surrounded by the sea.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time: October - April
  • Famous For: Pilgrimage & Pamban Bridge
  • Nearest Airport: Madurai
  • Languages: Tamil, English
