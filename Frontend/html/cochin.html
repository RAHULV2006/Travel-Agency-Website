Cochin

The Queen of the Arabian Sea

About Cochin

Cochin, also known as Kochi, is one of Kerala's most vibrant coastal cities. Famous for its historic Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, beautiful waterfronts and cultural heritage, Cochin is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

Chinese Fishing Nets

Historic fishing nets and iconic symbol of Kochi.

Fort Kochi

Colonial-era town with charming streets and heritage buildings.

Marine Drive

Popular waterfront promenade with stunning sunset views.

Quick Facts

  • State: Kerala
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Famous For: Fort Kochi & Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport
  • Languages: Malayalam, English
